Returns & Refunds

We offer a comprehensive 30-day return policy for your peace of mind:

Return Window: 30 days from delivery date

Condition Requirements:

  • Items must be in original condition and packaging
  • All accessories and documentation must be included
  • Items should be free from damage, wear, or modification
  • Original receipt or proof of purchase required

Non-Returnable Items:

  • Personalized or customized products
  • Software with broken seals
  • Items damaged by misuse or normal wear

Returning an item is simple and convenient:

Step 1: Initiate Return

  • Log into your account and go to "Order History"
  • Select the order and click "Return Items"
  • Choose items to return and select reason

Step 2: Print Return Label

  • We'll email you a prepaid return shipping label
  • Package items securely in original packaging
  • Attach the return label to the package

Step 3: Ship & Track

  • Drop off at any authorized shipping location
  • Track your return using the provided tracking number
  • Refund processed within 3-5 business days of receipt

Refund processing times depend on your payment method:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 3-5 business days after processing
  • PayPal: 1-2 business days after processing
  • Digital Wallets: 1-3 business days after processing
  • Bank Transfers: 5-7 business days after processing

We begin processing refunds within 24 hours of receiving your returned item in acceptable condition. You'll receive an email confirmation when your refund is processed.
