Warranty Terms and Conditions

What's Covered

Your Tech2Wear warranty covers manufacturing defects, hardware malfunctions, and component failures under normal use conditions. This includes:

  • Electronic component failures not caused by user damage
  • Manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship
  • Software-related issues that affect device functionality
  • Battery performance below specified standards (for applicable devices)
  • Connectivity and sensor malfunctions

What's Not Covered

The following situations are not covered under our standard warranty:

  • Physical damage from drops, impacts, or accidents
  • Water damage beyond specified IP ratings
  • Damage from misuse, abuse, or unauthorized modifications
  • Normal wear and tear, including cosmetic scratches
  • Damage from power surges or electrical issues
  • Issues caused by third-party software or accessories
  • Products purchased from unauthorized retailers

Warranty Period

Warranty coverage begins from the date of purchase and varies by product category as shown above. All warranty periods are clearly indicated on your purchase receipt and product documentation.

Proof of Purchase

To process warranty claims, you must provide valid proof of purchase including:

  • Original purchase receipt or invoice
  • Order confirmation email
  • Product serial number
  • Account information (if purchased online)

Important Information

Limitation of Liability

TO THE MAXIMUM EXTENT PERMITTED BY LAW: Tech2Wear's total liability for any warranty claim shall not exceed the purchase price of the product. We are not liable for indirect, incidental, special, or consequential damages including loss of data, profits, or business interruption.

International Warranty

Tech2Wear products are covered by international warranty. If you purchased your product in one country and need service in another, we'll honor your warranty coverage. Additional shipping costs may apply for international warranty claims.

Warranty Transfer

Warranties are transferable to new owners with proper documentation. Contact our support team to transfer warranty coverage when selling or gifting your Tech2Wear products.

Governing Law

This warranty is governed by the laws of the jurisdiction where the product was purchased. Any disputes will be resolved through binding arbitration.
